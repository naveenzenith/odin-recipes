Chicken-Chettinad

Description

Chicken Chettinad is a flavorful and spicy chicken curry that originates from the Chettinad region of Tamil Nadu.
It features a distinctive masala blend known as Chettinad masala, which includes a variety of spices.
The curry is enriched with coconut milk, giving it a creamy texture.
Chicken Chettinad is a delicious and aromatic dish often served with rice or Indian bread.

Ingredients

  • Chicken pieces (500g)
  • Chettinad masala powder (2 tbsp)
  • Coconut milk (1 cup)
