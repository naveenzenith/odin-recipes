Rasam

Description

Rasam is a spicy and tangy soup made with tamarind, tomatoes, and a blend of aromatic spices.
It is a light and soothing dish often served as a starter in South Indian meals.
Rasam is known for its refreshing taste and is believed to have digestive properties.
It can be served with rice or enjoyed on its own.

Ingredients

  • Tamarind pulp (1/4 cup)
  • Tomatoes (2, chopped)
  • Rasam powder (1 tbsp)

Steps

  1. Boil tamarind pulp, tomatoes, rasam powder, and salt in water.
  2. In a tadka pan, heat oil, add mustard seeds, cumin, and curry leaves.
  3. Pour tadka into the rasam and let it simmer.
  4. Garnish with coriander leaves and serve hot.
