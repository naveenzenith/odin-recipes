Dosa

Description

Dosa is a thin, crispy pancake made from a fermented batter of urad dal (black gram) and rice.
It is a popular South Indian breakfast item and is often served with coconut chutney and sambar.
Dosas come in various types, including plain dosa, masala dosa (filled with spiced potatoes), and more.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup urad dal (black gram)
  • 2 cups rice
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Soak urad dal and rice separately for 4-6 hours.
  2. Grind them into a smooth batter, mix, and add salt.
  3. Ferment the batter overnight.
  4. Heat a pan, spread the batter thinly, and cook until crisp.
  5. Serve with coconut chutney and sambar.
