Idli

Description

Idli is a soft and spongy steamed rice cake, made from a fermented batter of urad dal and idli rice.
This dish is a staple in South Indian households and is typically served with coconut chutney and sambar.
Idlis are not only delicious but also a healthy and easily digestible food.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup urad dal
  • 2 cups idli rice
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Soak urad dal and idli rice separately for 4-6 hours.
  2. Grind into a smooth batter, mix, and add salt.
  3. Ferment the batter overnight.
  4. Pour into idli molds and steam for 10-12 minutes
  5. Serve with coconut chutney and sambar
